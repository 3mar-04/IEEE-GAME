  • Why Mario In Jordan ?

    This game takes you on an interactive journey to explore Jordan's famous and unknown landmarks.

  • The Aim of this game ?

    Visit different locations in Jordan, complete unique challenges associated with each location, earn points and unlock new levels.

  • About this Game ?

    An interactive game that aims to promote tourism in Jordan through a new and fun experience that combines adventure and exploration. The game takes you on a virtual journey to explore the most prominent tourist attractions and distinctive historical and cultural places in Jordan.

  • Our Future ?

    Our future goal is to cover all the famous and unknown historical landmarks of Jordan such as Petra and the Dead Sea, and the civilized ones such as hotels, hospitals, government and sports centers.

  • Our Goal ?

    Increasing and supporting domestic and foreign tourism and strengthening the community's awareness of all historical and urban landmarks within Jordan in an entertaining manner.
